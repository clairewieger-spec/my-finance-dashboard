💡 Bonus Tax Heads-Up
Bonuses are typically withheld at a flat 22% federal rate. At your income level your marginal rate is likely higher — meaning you may owe the difference at tax time.

On a $9,000 bonus:
· Withheld at 22%: ~$1,980
· Nebraska state (~5%): ~$450
· Estimated take-home: ~$6,570

Set aside ~$500–700 extra from the bonus as a tax buffer, or adjust your W-4 withholding before the bonus hits.
